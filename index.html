Introducing Fountain of Wisdom

A village empowerment charity organization in Massachusetts, focused halfway across the world on a small village in central Kenya called Karia. We are dedicated to uplifting the status of an impoverished rural plantation village through the essential means of education — making permanent changes for the better.

Our singular goal is to improve learning in Karia by uplifting the substandard educational conditions. We'll do this by upgrading dilapidated classrooms, building a library and a computer lab, providing proper student desks, bringing up-to-date school materials, and constructing a sports field.

Read Our Story

Our Mission Statement

To improve learning in Karia primary school through upgrading dilapidated classrooms, building a library and computer lab, providing proper student desks and up-to-date learning materials, and improving sport fields. We also plan to build a community library to benefit village members who are not in school.
