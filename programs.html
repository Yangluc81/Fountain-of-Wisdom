Uplifting the substandard educational conditions

Our Ongoing Projects

In our modern world, the results of inadequate education run very deep. People who lack the necessary skills and mindsets find it difficult to keep up with change. Postponing education longer shrinks the chances of success to zero. We are not here to preach the danger but to display a ray of hope.

Our plans center on elevating the village of Karia from substandard conditions into a student-friendly learning environment. We currently have two ongoing projects. If we succeed here, we will leave an instructive mark on the village, its neighbors, and finally to Kenya as a whole.

First Project – Karia Primary School

Karia has only one primary school, and it is in poor condition. Our first project focuses on transforming this school into a proper learning environment. This includes:

  • Upgrading and rebuilding dilapidated classrooms
  • Providing proper student desks and chairs
  • Building a library and computer lab
  • Supplying up-to-date learning materials
  • Constructing a proper sports field
Support This Project

Second Project – Karia Community Library

Education doesn't end when the school bell rings. Our second project is to build a community library that serves all village members — not just those currently enrolled in school. This will:

  • Provide a knowledge center for the whole community
  • Offer books, periodicals, and reference materials
  • Support adult literacy and continuing education
  • Create a safe, peaceful space for learning
