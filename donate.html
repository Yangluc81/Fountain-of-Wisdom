Make a Difference

You are more than qualified to contribute to improving Karia and the Kenyan people. Every dollar you give goes directly toward education for children who need it most.

Your donation helps us:

  • Rebuild dilapidated classrooms
  • Build a school library and computer lab
  • Provide new desks and learning materials
  • Establish a community library for the village
